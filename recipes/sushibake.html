Favorite Recipe: Sushi Bake

This is my favorite recipe I've ever learned for the first time in high school. I used to make it for my friends all the time but the pandemic happened and I would make it whenever I wanted. It's really good and doesn't take a lot of effort.

Ingredients:

  • Sushi rice
  • Imitation Crab
  • Salmon
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • Sriracha
  • Cream Cheese
  • Furikake
  • Baking dish
  • Seaweed

Steps for cooking:

  1. Season salmon with paprika, salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  2. Place in airfryer for 10 minutes at 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  3. Once finished, shred imitation crab and salmon then place in a bowl
  4. Mix shredded proteins with kewpie mayo, sriracha and cream cheese until covered in desired amount
  5. Line baking dish with sushi rice
  6. Cover rice with furikake
  7. Place protein mixture over furikake sushi rice
  8. Top off with kewpie mayo, sriracha and furikake
  9. Place into oven for 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 20 minutes
  10. Take out, place it on a piece of seaweed and enjoy!
