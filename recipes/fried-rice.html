Fried Rice Recipe

Hi guys, today we'll be doing a recipe for fried rice, today we're using shrimp to remake Din Tai Fung

Ingredients:

  • Shrimp
  • White Rice
  • 2 Eggs
  • Scallions
  • Kosher Salt
  • White Pepper
  • MSG
  • Neutral Oil
  • Chicken Bouillon Powder

Steps for cooking:

  1. Marinade shrimp in kosher salt, white pepper, baking soda, and neutral oil.
  2. After marinating cook in medium high heat with 1 tbsp of neutral oil for 2-3 minutes until cooked through.
  3. Remove shrimp and saute scallion whites and cook eggs
  4. Add white rice and mixed thoroughly, incorporating egg and scallion with the rice while adding seasoning.
  5. Add shrimp and scallion greens.
