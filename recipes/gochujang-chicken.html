Gochujang Chicken Recipe

Airfried gochujang chicken thighs are the most juicy and delicious meal prep proteins you can add to your week.

Ingredients:

  • Gochujang
  • Gochugaru
  • Brown Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Seasoning - I use garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt and pepper
  • Chicken thighs

Steps for cooking:

  1. Combine all the ingredients but the chicken in a bowl
  2. Add the gochujang sauce marinade to the chicken thighs and mix until well coated. Let marinate in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Add a liner to the air fryer basket and add the chicken thighs to it in a single layer. Avoid overlapping
  4. Air fry at 400F for 10 minutes. Repeat with the rest of the chicken thighs until all done
